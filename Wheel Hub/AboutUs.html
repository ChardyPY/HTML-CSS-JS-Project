Who We Are

Welcome to Wheel Hub, a trusted name in automotive retail since 1995. With a legacy spanning over two decades, we have been dedicated to providing our customers with the finest selection of vehicles, unmatched customer service, and a commitment to quality. Our showrooms, strategically located across the United States, showcase a diverse range of cars, from sleek sedans to rugged SUVs, catering to the unique needs and preferences of every automotive enthusiast. At Wheel Hub, we believe in more than just selling cars; we strive to build lasting relationships with our customers, ensuring their journey with us is as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Our new online platform further extends this commitment, offering a seamless and innovative car shopping experience right at your fingertips. Join us at Wheel Hub, where your journey to finding the perfect car begins.

Second AutoFastCar Manufacturing Center In Jakarta

At Wheel Hub, our commitment to excellence begins at our state-of-the-art manufacturing center. Located in the heart of the automotive industry, our facility is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by a team of highly skilled engineers and technicians. This cutting-edge center is where innovation meets precision, ensuring that every vehicle we produce adheres to the highest standards of quality and performance. From the initial design phase to the final assembly, our manufacturing process is meticulously monitored and continuously improved to deliver cars that are not only reliable but also embody the spirit of innovation and craftsmanship. Our dedication to sustainability is evident in our eco-friendly manufacturing practices, which include the use of renewable energy sources and sustainable materials. At Wheel Hub, we are proud of our heritage and our role in driving the future of the automotive industry.

vision and mission

At Wheel Hub, our vision is to be the leading innovator in the automotive industry, setting the benchmark for quality, performance, and style in every vehicle we produce. We are dedicated to driving the future of sustainable transportation by integrating the latest technologies and eco-friendly practices into our manufacturing processes. Customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We strive to create an outstanding automotive shopping experience both in our showrooms and online. Innovation is a core value at Wheel Hub. We continuously seek to incorporate cutting-edge technologies, such as artificial intelligence and blockchain, to enhance our products and services. By focusing on these core areas, Wheel Hub is dedicated to creating exceptional vehicles and experiences that not only fulfill our customers' desires but also contribute to a more sustainable and innovative future in the automotive industry.
